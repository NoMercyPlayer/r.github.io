Simple & Easy Maths!
Here are simple Mathematics you should learn!
Fractions!
  • Fractions are partial numbers that represents a part or a portion of something
  • For example, 1/2 means 1 part out of 2. It can also be considered as 50% or 0.5
  • However, there are 3 types of fractions... Mixed Fractions, Proper Fractions, Improper Fractions
  • The upper number is called the numerator and the lower number is called a denominator
Mixed Fractions!
  • Mixed fractions contains a whole number and a partial number
  • E.g.: 1 1/2 represents 1 and a half (1.5) / (150%)
Proper Fractions!
  • Proper fractions are fractions with a numerator, that is smaller than the denominator
  • E.g.: 1/10 (0.1) (10%)
